Draw a cube on the screen. 
 
 
 
Amazing. 
 
Every frame we orbit the camera around a small amount 
creating the illusion of a spinning object.

a semi-unoptimized loop to draw the cube points onto the screen. 
 
OpenGL does all the hard work for us. :]

<span class="s3">def </span><span class="s1">perspective(fovy</span><span class="s3">, </span><span class="s1">aspect</span><span class="s3">, </span><span class="s1">znear</span><span class="s3">, </span><span class="s1">zfar):</span>

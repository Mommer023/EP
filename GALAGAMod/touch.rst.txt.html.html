      <span class="s0">x          the normalized x position of the finger, between 0 and 1.</span>
      <span class="s0">y          the normalized y position of the finger, between 0 and 1.</span>
      <span class="s0">pressure   the amount of pressure applied by the finger, between 0 and 1.</span>

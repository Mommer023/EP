I am so glad you're here! Let me tell you about myself and my journey to becoming a programmer! My passion has always been technology, from a very early age, I was fascinated with learning how to navigate the unknown within the evolving world of technology and software. As a child, I grew up watching the world slowly change from manual processes and minimal technological advancements, into a society that was built to thrive on a digital footprint. Every aspect of it was intriguing to me, which lead me to this path in my life. I began my journey after working in a role that utilitzed software that I felt wasn't as seamless as it could be. I was a processor and slowly realized that the capabilities of certain programs were minimal in some areas and advanced in others. I became interested in the integration of features from one platform to the next. I would love the opportunity to make an impact in the digital footprint. If you would like to know more, please review the information available throughout my site. Thank you for stopping by!
